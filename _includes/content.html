About me

My name is Joschka Huellmann and I am currently working for the Smarter Work competence center of the ERCIS at the Westfälische Wilhelms-University Münster.

I research algorithms and networks in the future workplace and explore patterns of communication and collaboration practices. I promote the digital workplace and enable organisations to understand and seize the opportunities presented to them by the digital workplace.
