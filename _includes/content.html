About me

My name is Joschka Hüllmann and I am currently working for the Smarter Work competence center of the ERCIS at the Westfälische Wilhelms-University Münster.

My research focuses on digital traces from communication and collaboration software in organisations. In particular, I explore patterns of collaboration that yield satisfying and productive work practices. I strive to promote the digital workplace and enable organisations to understand and seize the opportunities presented to them by the workplace of the future.

Besides writing academic articles, I am interested in maths, coding, popular science, edutainment, short stories, and the craft of writing. As such, I contribute to pop-journals, publish articles on my blog, and push my commits to open-source projects.
